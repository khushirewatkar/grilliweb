About GRILLI

Welcome to Grilzz, where culinary excellence meets exceptional hospitality. Our story is one of passion, dedication, and a deep love for food.

Our Journey

In the heart of London, Grilzz was established in 2005 by Christoff Meadows, a culinary enthusiast with a dream to create a dining experience like no other. What started as a small, family-run eatery has blossomed into a renowned destination for food lovers.

Our Philosophy

At Grilzz, we believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's an art form. Our philosophy revolves around using the finest, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that tantalize your taste buds and warm your heart. We take pride in crafting a menu that showcases the diversity of flavors, paying homage to both tradition and innovation.

Our Team

Behind every great meal is a great team. Our chefs, sommeliers, and servers are passionate individuals who share a common goal: to make your dining experience unforgettable. Meet the creative minds who turn our culinary dreams into reality.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainability and the community. We work closely with local farmers, striving to reduce our carbon footprint while supporting those who feed our tables. Our commitment to the environment goes hand in hand with our dedication to creating memorable dining experiences for our guests.
